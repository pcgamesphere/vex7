Advanced Techniques in Vex 7

Once you've mastered the basics, you can start incorporating these advanced techniques into your Vex 7 gameplay:

1. Wall Sliding

Hold against a wall while falling to slow your descent. This technique is essential for navigating vertical sections and can save you from falling into hazards.

2. Double Jump Timing

Learn to maximize your jump height by timing your second jump at the peak of your first jump. This gives you extra height and distance.

3. Momentum Conservation

Keep your speed up by landing at the right angles and avoiding unnecessary stops. Maintaining momentum can help you clear longer gaps and reach distant platforms.

4. Quick Direction Changes

Master the art of quickly changing direction mid-air. This skill is crucial for avoiding last-second obstacles and making tight maneuvers.
